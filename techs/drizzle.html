Drizzle

A type‑safe SQL toolkit that's growing on me

I mostly use Prisma, but lately I’ve been learning Drizzle and starting to appreciate its flexibility. It’s simpler than it looks, and I like how close it feels to writing raw SQL—just with type safety baked in. Still early days for me, but I’m already seeing where it could shine.

  • Typed schemas, clean migrations, no magic
  • Good fit for lightweight or edge-first apps

Still experimenting, but I wouldn’t be surprised if Drizzle becomes a regular in my stack.
