Express Rate Limit

My go-to middleware to protect APIs from abuse and brute force.

When I’m building with Express, I always add rate limiting to sensitive routes like login, contact forms, or auth endpoints. It’s lightweight, easy to configure, and gives instant protection without much effort.

  • Helps stop spam and brute-force attempts
  • Easy to set global or route-specific limits

A simple layer of defense that adds serious value to any Express app.
