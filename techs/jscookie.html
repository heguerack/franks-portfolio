js-cookie

A tiny library I use when I want to handle cookies fast and pain-free.

Whether I’m setting tokens, saving preferences, or managing simple client-side state, js-cookie keeps it clean and readable. No need to mess with `document.cookie` or remember encoding rules. I’ve used it mostly when I’m not working with a full auth system or just need a quick way to store lightweight data on the client.

  • Simple API: `Cookies.set`, `Cookies.get`, `Cookies.remove`
  • Great for auth flows, themes, and client-side flags

When localStorage isn’t the right fit, js-cookie is a solid alternative.
