Netlify

Along with Vercel, it's one of my go-to platforms for static websites.

Netlify handles deployment, HTTPS, and CI/CD out of the box. I just push code to GitHub, and it goes live. It’s ideal for JAMstack sites, and I use it for smaller frontend projects and demo builds.

  • Push-to-deploy workflow keeps things fast and simple
  • Includes serverless functions, form handling, and more

Most of my smaller sites — like this portfolio — have been deployed on Netlify at some point.
