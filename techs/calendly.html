React Calendly

A simple wrapper to drop Calendly into my React projects.

I use React Calendly when I want to embed my booking link directly into a page — whether it's a contact section or a call-to-action. It’s clean, quick to implement, and keeps users on-site instead of sending them somewhere else.

  • Keeps everything React-friendly with minimal setup
  • Perfect for landing pages or dashboards that need inline scheduling

It’s one of those tools that just makes things smoother — for me and the people booking.
