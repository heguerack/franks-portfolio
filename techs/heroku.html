Heroku

The first platform I used to deploy full-stack apps — quick, simple, and beginner‑friendly.

I used Heroku when I was just getting into backend work. It handled my Node/Express apps with zero config, and taught me how deployment actually works. I still use it sometimes for quick demos or testing APIs without needing a full cloud setup.

  • Simple CLI, free tier, and easy Git-based deploys
  • Good intro to environment variables, scaling, and logs

Heroku was my gateway into DevOps — still useful when I need to spin up something fast.
